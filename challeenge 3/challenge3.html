Diagnosing Foliage Problems

Here's a quick guide to diagnosing common problems with plant foilage

  1. Yellow
    • Wet soil
    • Low fertility
    • Root rot pathogens
    • Roots cut or damaged
      • Gopherrs
      • Moles
      • Insects
    • Nematodes
  2. Necrotic spots
  3. White powder
  4. Light and dark green areas
  5. Holes or chewed areas
